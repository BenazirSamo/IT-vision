Michelin-Starred Cuisine

Our award-winning chefs craft each dish with precision and passion, using only the finest ingredients sourced from trusted local producers and international markets.

Curated Wine Cellar

With over 1,200 selections, our master sommelier will guide you to the perfect pairing from our extensive collection of international and rare vintages.

Luxurious Ambiance

Designed by renowned architects, our dining spaces feature custom furnishings, designer lighting, and impeccable attention to every aesthetic detail.

Chef's Signature Creation

Truffle-Infused Wagyu Beef

Our most celebrated dish features A5 Japanese Wagyu beef, aged for 28 days and slow-cooked to perfection, infused with black truffle essence from Piedmont, served with seasonal vegetables and our signature reduction sauce.

This masterpiece is available as part of our tasting menu or à la carte. Each serving is accompanied by a sommelier-selected wine pairing to enhance the flavors.
