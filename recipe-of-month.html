Gourmet Mushroom Risotto

45 mins 4 servings Medium difficulty

Ingredients

  • Arborio Rice - 1.5 cups
  • Mixed Mushrooms (cremini, shiitake, oyster) - 500g
  • Vegetable Broth - 4 cups
  • Dry White Wine - 1/2 cup
  • Freshly Grated Parmesan Cheese - 1 cup
  • Shallots - 2 medium, finely diced
  • Garlic - 3 cloves, minced
  • Fresh Thyme - 2 tsp
  • Unsalted Butter - 3 tbsp
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil - 2 tbsp
  • Salt and Freshly Ground Black Pepper to taste
  • Truffle Oil (optional) - for drizzling

Instructions

  1. Heat the vegetable broth in a saucepan over medium heat and keep warm.
  2. In the same pan, add remaining oil and butter. Sauté shallots until translucent, about 3 minutes. Add garlic and cook for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  3. Add the Arborio rice and toast for 2 minutes, stirring constantly, until the grains are slightly translucent at the edges.
  4. Deglaze with white wine, stirring until completely absorbed.
  5. When the rice is al dente and creamy, stir in the cooked mushrooms and parmesan cheese. Adjust seasoning if needed.
  6. Remove from heat, cover, and let rest for 2 minutes before serving. Drizzle with truffle oil if desired.
