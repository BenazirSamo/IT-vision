Our Culinary Journey

Discover the passion, dedication, and artistry behind TASTNEST's Michelin-starred dining experience

2010

Our Humble Beginnings

What began as a small 30-seat bistro in 2010 has blossomed into one of the world's most celebrated dining destinations. Founded by Chef Marco Vincenzo and restaurateur Elena Petrov, TASTNEST was born from a shared vision to create a culinary sanctuary where innovation meets tradition.

Our first location in New York's vibrant culinary scene quickly gained recognition for its bold flavors and impeccable service. Within two years, we earned our first Michelin star, a testament to our team's relentless pursuit of excellence.

Today, TASTNEST stands as a three-Michelin-starred establishment, ranked among the World's 50 Best Restaurants. Yet we remain true to our founding principles: respect for ingredients, mastery of technique, and creating unforgettable moments for our guests.

Marco Vincenzo & Elena Petrov
