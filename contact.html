Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about reservations, dietary requirements, and more

Reservations can be made online through our website, by calling +1 (212) 555-7878, or via email at reservations@tastnest.com. We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance for weekend dining.

We require 24 hours notice for cancellation of reservations. For parties of 6 or more, we require 48 hours notice. Late cancellations or no-shows may be subject to a $50 per person fee.

Yes, our chefs are happy to accommodate dietary restrictions with advance notice. Please inform us of any allergies or dietary requirements when making your reservation. We can accommodate gluten-free, vegetarian, vegan, and most other dietary needs.

We recommend smart casual attire. While we don't require jackets for gentlemen, we do ask guests to avoid overly casual clothing such as shorts, flip-flops, or athletic wear. For our Chef's Table experience, business casual or cocktail attire is preferred.

Yes, we have several private dining options available for groups from 10 to 50 guests. Our private dining coordinator would be happy to discuss menus and availability. Please contact our events team at events@tastnest.com or call +1 (212) 555-7879 for more information.
